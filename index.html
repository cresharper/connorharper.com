I'm Connor - a Web and Digital Professional

Who I am View My Work

A little bit about me:

I am a Web Developer and Designer with 3 years of experience helping busniesses and individuals acheive their goals related to digital presence. I've taken on projects ranging from making landing pages for local bands to developing the Front End for E-Commerce sites with 500+ products. There isn't an assingment that scares me, and whatever I don't know, I will learn and get the job done.

My Work

Dynatrace

At my current full time position, Dynatrace, I work closely with the marketing team to design and develop an effective user experience for the Dynatrace website that drives conversions through the site. I Collaborate closely with the development team to write scalable and clean code along with working with the Graphic Designers to build out the Front End. In March 2016 I led the front end aspect of the website relaunch and was responsible for the responsive design of the website.
